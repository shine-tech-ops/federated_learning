基于Flower的整体联邦学习时序图

sequenceDiagram participant EdgeDevice as 边缘设备 (Flower Client) participant RegionalServer as 区域聚合服务器 (Flower Server & Client) participant CentralServer as 中央服务器 (Flower Server) participant ModelStorage as 模型存储 Note over CentralServer, ModelStorage: 阶段一：系统启动与初始模型下发 CentralServer->>ModelStorage: 1. 获取初始全局模型 ModelStorage-->>CentralServer: 2. 返回初始全局模型 CentralServer->>RegionalServer: 3. (Flower) 下发初始全局模型参数 / 启动指令 RegionalServer->>EdgeDevice: 4. (Flower) 下发初始全局模型参数 / 任务指令 Note over EdgeDevice, ModelStorage: 阶段二：联邦学习训练轮次循环 loop 每一轮训练 CentralServer->>CentralServer: 5. (Flower Server) 启动全局聚合轮次 (e.g., `start_round`) CentralServer->>RegionalServer: 6. (Flower Request) 请求区域聚合模型 (e.g., `GetParameters`) RegionalServer->>RegionalServer: 7. (Flower Server) 启动区域内客户端选择与协调 RegionalServer->>EdgeDevice: 8. (Flower Request) 下发当前模型参数 (e.g., `FitIns`) EdgeDevice->>EdgeDevice: 9. (Flower Client) 在本地数据上训练模型 EdgeDevice->>RegionalServer: 10. (Flower Return) 上传本地模型更新 (参数差值, 样本数) RegionalServer->>RegionalServer: 11. (Flower Server) 接收并聚合边缘设备更新 (e.g., `FedAvg`) RegionalServer->>CentralServer: 12. (Flower Return) 上传区域聚合模型 (作为中央服务器的Client) CentralServer->>CentralServer: 13. (Flower Server) 接收并聚合区域聚合模型 CentralServer->>ModelStorage: 14. 存储最新全局模型 ModelStorage-->>CentralServer: 15. 存储成功 CentralServer->>RegionalServer: 16. (Flower Response) 下发最新全局模型参数 (用于下一轮区域聚合) RegionalServer->>EdgeDevice: 17. (Flower Response) 下发最新全局模型参数 (用于下一轮边缘训练) end
