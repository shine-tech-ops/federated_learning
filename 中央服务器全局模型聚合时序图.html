基于Flower的中央服务器全局模型聚合时序图

sequenceDiagram participant RegionalServer as 区域聚合服务器 (Flower Client for Central) participant CentralServer as 中央服务器 (Flower Server) participant ModelStorage as 模型存储 Note over CentralServer, ModelStorage: 全局聚合过程 CentralServer->>CentralServer: 1. (Flower Server) 启动全局聚合轮次 CentralServer->>RegionalServer: 2. 请求区域聚合模型 (GetAggregatedModel) RegionalServer->>CentralServer: 3. 上传区域聚合模型 (AggregatedWeights, NumSamples) CentralServer->>CentralServer: 4. 接收多个区域聚合模型更新 CentralServer->>CentralServer: 5. (Flower Server) 执行全局模型聚合 (如FedAvg) CentralServer->>ModelStorage: 6. 存储最新全局模型 ModelStorage-->>CentralServer: 7. 存储成功 CentralServer->>RegionalServer: 8. (可选) 下发最新全局模型参数 (用于下一层区域聚合) RegionalServer-->>RegionalServer: (准备下一轮区域聚合)
